Research interests

My research interest lies in teh hardware-and-software codesign of an energy-efficient reconfigurable hardware system design targetting artificial intellegence based applications. On the software side, I am working on optimizing spiking neural works for fast and energy efficient information processing. On the hardware side, I am developing a digital neuromorphic hardware system with a reconfigurable architecture and energy-efficient event communication scheme.
